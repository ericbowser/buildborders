## What plaque sellers actually cut

Bordered awards, oval desk signs, and medallion coasters share needs:

- **Vector cut** for outer frame (clean corners, predictable kerf)
- **Engrave** for center text or logo
- **Repeatable sizing** — 5×7, 8×10, 3″ round

Your machine choice is less about maximum power and more about **bed size, enclosure, and software workflow**.

## Our picks by budget

### Shop-ready CO₂ — [xTool P2](/review/xtool-p2)

Enclosed 55W, camera alignment, pass-through. Best when borders are daily production, not weekend experiments.

**Compare:** [xTool P2 vs OMTech 55W](/compare/xtool-p2/omtech-55w)

### Value CO₂ — [OMTech 55W](/review/omtech-55w)

Open frame, strong community, LightBurn-first. Best if you already have exhaust sorted and want lower entry cost.

### Budget diode — [xTool S1](/review/xtool-s1)

Large bed diode for learning bordered Etsy listings. Slower on thick acrylic; great for wood double-line frames.

### Easy cloud UI — [Glowforge Pro](/review/glowforge-pro)

Less parametric control for kerf-aware borders, but approachable for designers avoiding LightBurn.
